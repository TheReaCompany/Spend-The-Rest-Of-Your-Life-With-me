AIR BnB

Never heard of it you say? You will. It's a very affordable and imersive way to rent someone's entire apartment, loft, studio or just a room.

LE JOLIE

Hotel in Williamsburg, with a great view of the BQE (Brooklyn Queens Expressway) and close to our apartment. Although we never stayed here, it comes recommended by friends and is fairly reasonable.

THE ACE HOTEL

In NYC and bit of a scene, but has a Stumptown coffee downstairs. A little pricey and in an upcoming area called NOMAD because it is north of Madison Park. Also close to the office where the romance began.

The GERSHWIN HOTEL

This place is funky and for those with more adventrous tastes. They definitely have rates that are reasonable for the intense artistic vibe this place exudes. Not for the faint of heart.
